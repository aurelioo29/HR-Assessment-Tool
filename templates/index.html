Employee Attrition Insight is an analysis used to understand why employees leave or stay. This insight helps companies make strategic decisions to improve employee retention.

  • Stayed (0) Employees who remain with the company.
  • Leaves (1) Employees who leave the company.

    The main objectives are:
  • To identify factors that influence employee retention and attrition.
  • To provide insights to the company to improve employee satisfaction.
  • To reduce costs due to high employee turnover.

The prediction is done using a machine learning model that analyzes various parameters such as age, salary, job satisfaction level, overtime, and work experience.
* employee satisfaction in the work environment
